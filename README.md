# LP (Life Project) - An Ecosystem Simulation Game

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)](LICENSE)

Welcome to LP, a unique 2D platformer game that simulates a living and breathing ecosystem. In LP, players step into the role of various animals to explore and interact with a dynamic world teeming with life, where every element is intricately interconnected.

## Features

- **Realistic Ecosystem**: Explore an ecosystem where animals, plants, weather, and natural phenomena interact realistically, just like in the natural world.

- **Modular Design**: Customize and adapt every element in the game, from animals to environmental features, for a unique and flexible gaming experience.

- **Dynamic Evolution**: Watch species adapt and evolve based on environmental conditions and player interactions.

- **AI-Controlled Entities**: Experience a living, breathing world where AI-controlled animals and plants lead their own lives with depth and authenticity.

- **Complex Systems**: Manipulate terrain, observe weather patterns, and manage resources as you immerse yourself in a complex and immersive gameplay experience.
